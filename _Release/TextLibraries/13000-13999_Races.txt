13000|Humans are a versatile race of people, mostly known for their striking examples of adapting to odd environments and being driven to do so by enormous ambition. Those who dislike humans tend to view them as short sighted and self important.\n\nYour ability scores each increase by 1.\n\nHumans reach adulthood in their late teens and live less than a century.\n\nHumans tend toward no particular alignment. The best and the worst are found among them.\n\nHumans vary widely in height and build, from barely 5 feet tall to well over 6 feet tall (4 tiles). Regardless of your position in that range, your size is Medium.\n\nYour base walking speed is 30 feet (6 tiles).\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and one extra language of your choice. Humans typically learn the languages of other peoples they deal with, including obscure dialects. They are fond of sprinkling their speech with words borrowed from other tongues: Orc curses, Elvish musical expressions, Dwarvish military phrases, and so on.
13001|Elves are a thin, refined race of people native to the forest, mostly known for their art, music, and patience, often building grand cities through the cultivation and protection of especially long-lived trees. Those who dislike elves tend to view them as distant and arrogant, unwilling to bother with the trivialities of the other races.\n\nYour Dexterity score increases by 2 and your Intelligence score increases by 1.\n\nAlthough elves reach physical maturity at about the same age as humans, the elven understanding of adulthood goes beyond physical growth to encompass worldly experience. An elf typically claims adulthood and an adult name at around hte age of 100 and can live to be 750 years old.\n\nElves love freedom, variety, and self-expression, so they lean strongly toward the gentler aspects of chaos. They value and protect others' freedom as well as their own, and they are more often good than not.\n\nElves range from under 5 feet to over 6 feet tall (4 tiles) and have slender builds. Your size is Medium.\n\nYour base walking speed is 30 feet (6 tiles).Accustomed to twilit forests and the night sky, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.\n\nYou have proficiency in the Perception skill.\n\nYou have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.\n\nElves don't need to sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply, remaining semiconscious, for 4 hours a day. (The common word for such meditation is "trance.") While meditating, you can dream after a fashion; such dreams are actually mental exercises that have become reflexive through years of practice. After resting this way, you gain the same benefit that a human does from 8 hours of sleep.\n\nYou have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.\n\nYou know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and Elvish, as well as one extra language of your choice. Elven literature is rich and varied, and their songs and poems are famous among other races. Many bards learn their language so they can add Elvish ballads to their repertoires.
13002|Dwarves are a short, stocky, and practical people native to the hills and mountains, mostly known for their great works of metal and stone. Though few commen especially on dwarven ambition, the stories of the race seem to include this, often of dwarves 'delving too deep' and struggling with the consequence, which tends to be a favorite citation of those who dislike dwarves.\n\nYour Constitution score increases by 2 and your Wisdom score increases by 1.\n\nDwarves mature at the same rate as humans, but they're considered young until they reach the age of 50. On average, they live about 350 years.\n\nMost dwarves are lawful, believing firmly in the benefits of a wll-ordered society. They tend toward good as well, with a strong sense of fair play and a belief that everyone deserves to share in the benefits of a just order.\n\nDwarves stand between 4 and 5 feet tall (3 tiles) and average about 150 pounds. Your size is Medium.\n\nYour base walking speed is 25 feet. Your speed is not reduced by wearing heavy armor.\n\nAccustomed to life underground, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.\n\nYou have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.\n\nYou have proficiency with the battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, and warhammer.\n\nYou gain proficiency with your choice of Smithing, Brewing, or Masonry.\n\nWhenever you make an Intelligence (History) check related to the origin of stonework, you are considered proficient in the History skill and add double your proficiency bonus to the check, instead of your normal proficiency bonus.\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and Dwarvish. Dwarvish is full of hard consonants and guttural sounds, and those characteristics spill over into whatever other language a dwarf might speak.\n\nYour hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 more than a level would otherwise grant every time you gain a level.
13003|Halflings are small and humble people focused on the home and hearth, rarely holding any goals outside of the security of their home town and their source of food. A rare few manage irrational hatred of halflings, but most simply forget that they exist until reminded.\n\nYour Dexterity score increases by 2 and your Charisma score increases by 1.\n\nA halfling reaches adulthood at the age of 20 and generally lives into the middle of his or her second century.\n\nMost halflings are lawful good. As a rule, they are good-hearted and kind, hate to see others in pain, and have no tolerance for oppression. They are also very orderly and traditional, leaning heavily on the support of their community and hte comfort of their old ways.\n\nHalflings average about 3 feet tall (2 tiles) and weigh about 40 pounds. Your size is Small.\n\nYour base walking speed is 25 feet (5 tiles).\n\nWhen you roll a 1 on the d20 for an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.\n\nYou have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.\n\nYou can move through the space of any creature that is of a size larger than yours.\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and Halfling. The Halfling language isn't secret, but halflings are loath to share it with others. They write very little, so they don't have a rich body of literature. Their oral tradition, however, is very strong. Almost all halflings speak Common to converse with the people in whose lands they dwell or through which they're traveling.\n\nYou can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you.
13004|Gnomes are a small and creative people native to the underground, known mostly for their great engineering projects, particularly mechanical devices. However, those who dislike gnomes tend to reference the destructive potential of these projects when they fail, which tends to involve a lot of collateral damage.\n\nYour Intelligence score increases by 2 and your Constitution score increases by 1.\n\nGnomes mature at the same rate humans do, and most are expected to settle down into an adult life by around age 40. They live about four centuries.\n\nGnomes are most often good. Those who tend toward law are sages, engineers, researchers, scholars, investigators, or investors. Those who tend toward chaos are minstrels, tricksters, wanderers, or fanciful jewelers. Gnomes are good-hearted, and even the tricksters among them are more playful than vicious.\n\nGnomes are between 3 and 4 feet tall (2 tiles) and average about 40 pounds. Your size is Small.\n\nYour base walking speed is 25 feet (5 tiles).\n\nAccustomed to life underground, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.\n\nYou have advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic.\n\nWhenever you make an Intelligence (History) check related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices, you can add twice you proficiency bonus, instead of any proficiency bonus you normally apply.\n\nYou have proficiency at Tinkering.\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and Gnomish. The Gnomish language, which uses the Dwarvish script, is renowned for its technical treatises and its catalogs of knowledge about the natural world.
13005|Half-elves are people with mixed heritage between elf and human. They typically make up a minority population in both communities where the two races interact, though their own reputation tends to be subsumed into whatever culture they grew up with.\n\nYour Charisma score increases by 2, and two other ability scores of your choice increase by 1.\n\nHalf-elves mature at the same rate humans do and reach adulthood around the age of 20. They live much longer than humans, however, often exceeding 180 years.\b\bHalf-elves share the chaotic bent of their elven heritage. They value both personal freedom and creative expressoin, demonstrating neither love of leaders nor desire for followers. They chafe at rules, resent others' demands, and sometimes prove unreliable, or at least unpredictable.\n\nHalf-elves are about the same size as humans, ranging from 5 to 6 feet tall (4 tiles). Your size is Medium.\n\nYour base walking speed is 30 feet (6 tiles).\n\nThanks to your elf blood, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.\n\nYou have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.\n\nYou gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.\n\nYou can speak read, and write Common, Elvish, and one extra language of your choice.
13006|Half-orcs are people with mixed heritage between orc and human. They typically make up a minority population in both communities where the two races interact. Unlikely half-elves, half-orcs tend to have an extremely-unfavorable reputation in both communities, either seen as weak freeloaders among orcs or savage invaders by humans.\n\nYour Strength score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 1.\n\nHalf-orcs mature a little faster than humans, reaching adulthood around age 14. They age noticeably faster and rarely live longer than 75 years.\n\nHalf-orcs inherit a tendency toward chaos from their orc parents are are not strongly inclined toward good. Half-orcs raised among orcs and willing to live out their lives among them are usually evil.\n\nHalf-orcs are somewhat larger and bulkier than humans, and they range from 5 to well over 6 feet tall (4 tiles). Your size is Medium.\n\nYour base walking speed is 30 feet (6 tiles).\n\nThanks to your orc blood, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.\n\nYou gain proficiency in the Intimidation skill.\n\nWhen you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hit point instead. You can't use this feature again until you finish a long rest.\n\nWhen you score a critical hit with a melee weapon attack, you can roll one of the weapon's damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.\n\nYou can speak, read, and write Common and Orc. Orc is a harsh, grating language with hard consonants. It has no script of its own but is written in the Dwarvish script.
